About Us

Yabash Restaurant found in 2022 follows the tale of a renowned Nigerian cook, Iya Bashira otherwise known as Bashira's mum, who is known for the uniqueness in her cooking style among other Nigerian Dishes.

Yabash's foundation lies upon delivering Iya Bashira's special taste after learning the secret to her tantalizing effect. Moreso, Yabash brings her customers a conducive environment where they can comfortably enjoy Nigerian dishes without any hassle.
