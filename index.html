About Us

Yabash kitchen follows the renowned Nigerian cook tale, Iya Bashira, otherwise known as Bashira's mum who is nown for her uniqueness incooking among other Nigerian Dishes. Yabash's foundation lies upon the reliance delivering Iya Bashira's special taste after learning the secret to her tantalizing effect. Yabash in addition, brings her customers a conducive environment where they can comfortably enjoy their Nigerian meal without any hassle.
